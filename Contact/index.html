Join us

Our team is scientifically oriented, and the content of research is closely integrated with the practical application, focusing on the cultivation of students’ abilities. The atmosphere of our team is free and academic, and the lab is fully equipped (including graphic workstations, large servers, and high-performance PCs). Our lab respects for students’ autonomy and chooses the research direction according to the students’ wishes. The time allocation is free and there is no compulsory, and the academic exchange opportunities are sufficient. The lab welcomes students with strong programming skills and solid foundation in mathematics and English. Those with scientific research experience or academic achievements are preferred.
