The Information Fusion & Data Mining Lab is affiliated to the School of Big Data and Software of Chongqing University. It currently has 14 members, including 1 teacher, 9 master students, 3 undergraduates and 1 international student. In the past few years, the laboratory has graduated 11 students, including 1 doctor and 10 masters.
